## Brand Fit Ratings

- **AutoProxy: Perfect.** The tagline made into software. Counter-positioned against every AI messaging tool. The market gap is real and defensible.
- **Stacker: Strong.** Composable domain architecture and single-business philosophy align cleanly with Right-Sized by Design and Self-Sufficiency. Risk is scope -- 12+ domains is a lot of surface for one builder.

## Hospitality Tension Resolution

Lead with hospitality. Never apologize for the vertical. Expand deliberately.

- Vertical credibility converts to horizontal currency
- Hospitality is a pressure cooker -- if tools work there, they work anywhere
- The domain architecture already enables expansion without a rebrand
- Soloscaler brand itself never narrows to hospitality -- it's the umbrella

## AutoProxy Strategic Insight

AutoProxy should launch first, not Stacker. It is smaller, universally relatable, and the single most brand-aligned product. "275 interruptions a day. AutoProxy is not another AI tool that helps you do more. It's the first one that protects you from doing things you should never have been asked to do."

Should be both: a native Stacker integration AND a standalone product for Slack/Teams/email. The standalone version is where the brand story lives. The Stacker integration is where the flywheel lives.

## New Product Recommendations

### 1. Sentinel
Automated brand and operations monitoring -- watches your digital presence, vendor SLAs, review platforms, and key metrics. Tells you only when something actually needs attention. The "guardian while you sleep" product. **Complexity: Moderate (8-12 weeks MVP).**

### 2. Playbook
Living operations manual builder -- turns workflows, SOPs, and tribal knowledge into a searchable, executable system. Self-Sufficiency as the Goal turned into a product. Natural deliverable format for consulting work. **Complexity: Moderate-High (10-14 weeks MVP).**

### 3. Cashview
Real-time financial clarity layer -- connects bank, payment processor, and invoicing to deliver one daily answer: "Here is where your money is and where it is going." Not a full accounting system, just the view that matters. **Complexity: Moderate (10-14 weeks MVP, compliance extends).**

### 4. Dealflow
Minimalist CRM for people who hate CRMs -- assumes sales is one of twelve things you do today. Interface is a daily briefing, not a database. "Here are the three deals that need you today." **Complexity: Low-Moderate (6-10 weeks MVP).**

## Go-to-Market Sequence

| Phase | Months | Launch | Rationale |

| 1: Wedge | 1-4 | AutoProxy (standalone + Stacker integration) | Smallest, sharpest, most brand-aligned |

| 3: Expand | 8-14 | Sentinel + Cashview | Monitoring + financial clarity layers |
