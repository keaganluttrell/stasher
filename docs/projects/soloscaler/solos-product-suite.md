## Overview

SolOS is the Soloscaler Operating System. Its logo is a Sun. The platform consists of five core tools that work together to give soloscalers and small businesses full control over their data, their time, and their operations.

The stack solves one core problem: **time reclamation**. Whether you are a soloscaler fielding client questions or a startup bleeding hours to shoulder-tapping and disorganization, SolOS eliminates the noise and keeps the signal strong.

## Stasher — The Knowledge Mine

Stasher is the knowledge mine of SolOS. It is internal-only — nothing outside reads from it directly.

Raw files drop into an inbox. Agents process them using Zettelkasten principles: extracting atomic notes, linking related concepts, and discarding fluff. What remains is a dense, interconnected store of facts — not documents, not folders, but atoms of information that can be queried and composed into coherent answers.

Stasher is the primary resource that AutoProxy draws from when answering on your behalf. It is what Staffer's agents reference when doing work. Every tool in the stack gets smarter because Stasher exists. Without it, the rest of SolOS is guessing.

## Staffer — The Workforce

Staffer is the workforce of SolOS. It operates like a staffing agency — you submit a request, and Staffer dispatches the right agents to get it done.

Behind the scenes, Staffer manages a pool of specialized agents waiting on a work call. You don't need to know which agent does what. You describe the job, Staffer assigns the labor, and the work gets completed. Agents pull from Stasher for context, post results to Stacker, and report back when the job is done.

Staffer is how a soloscaler operates like a ten-person team without hiring anyone.

## Stacker — The Command Center

Stacker is the command center of SolOS. It is a domain-driven platform that understands your business problems and builds solutions right-sized to your operations.

At its core, Stacker gives you channels, chats, and tasks — the two things every business needs. But unlike Slack and Asana, they live in the same system. You talk about the work on the work. You field internal questions from your team, dispatch agents from Staffer to pick up tasks, have reports written back to Stasher, and let AutoProxy handle messages on your behalf. Accountability is built in.

Stacker is also the hub that every other tool in the stack talks through. Staffer dispatches work here. Stasher feeds context in. AutoProxy routes and responds through it. You stack your business domains into the system, and the signal stays strong. No bloat. No noise. It runs lean.

## Static — The Brand Layer

Static is your brand. It is how you present yourself to the world — who you are, what you do, and why it matters.

Static produces marketing pages, blogs, content, and information sites. These are high-signal, low-drag static pages that represent you and your business. While Stasher is what you know, Static is what you show.

AutoProxy can field questions directly from a chat widget on your Static site, giving visitors real answers drawn from your knowledge base without pulling you away from your work. Your public face stays active even when you don't.

## AutoProxy — The Superpower

AutoProxy allows you to work distraction-free. As a soloscaler, you cannot answer every message, email, call, or text. AutoProxy is your defense. It has deep access to your Stasher, your Stacker, your Static, and your team. It determines if something is worth your time or can wait. It routes messages to the right place, or it answers the request straight from the knowledge base.

AutoProxy is the product that sells itself. The client who texts you a question, figures it out two minutes later, and texts again to say never mind — that interaction never reaches you. AutoProxy handled it before you even knew it happened.

## How the Stack Connects

- **Stasher** is the brain. Everything reads from it.
- **Staffer** is the hands. It does the work.
- **Stacker** is the nerve center. Everything flows through it.
- **Static** is the face. It shows the world who you are.
- **AutoProxy** is the shield. It protects your time and answers on your behalf.

## The Thesis

This stack is not revolutionary because of what is new. It is revolutionary because of what it gives back — your time, your data, and your control.

Other platforms can do pieces of this. But when you use them, you play nice with SaaS. Your access can get dropped, terminated, or neutered. It is your data trapped in their software.

SolOS flips that. Your data. Your software. Your agents. Your rules. Full signal, no bull.
