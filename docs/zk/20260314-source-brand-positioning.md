## Key Takeaways

- Soloscaler occupies the gap between DIY chaos and unaffordable enterprise solutions for $200K-$5M businesses
- The core tension is three-sided: freelancers lack strategy, agencies create dependency, SaaS tools are enterprise-lite
- Four positioning pillars: Proof Over Promise, Right-Sized Not Down-Sized, Leverage Not Labor, Independence Over Dependency
- The target is operators technical enough to appreciate smart systems but too busy to build them
- One-sentence thesis: one person as effective as ten through systems, tools, and strategy
