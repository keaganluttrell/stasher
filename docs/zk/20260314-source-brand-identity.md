## Key Takeaways

- Five core values anchoring every brand decision: Leverage Over Labor, Radical Honesty, Builder's Mentality, Right-Sized by Design, Self-Sufficiency as the Goal
- Brand personality is "The Skilled Ally" — experienced builder who sits next to you, not the guru on the mountain
- Voice is professional but human, opinionated, concrete, and time-respecting
- Primary tagline "One person. Unfair advantage." chosen for versatility and intrigue
- Four messaging pillars map directly to the positioning pillars
