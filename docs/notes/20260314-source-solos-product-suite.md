## Key Takeaways

- SolOS is positioned as an operating system, not a tool suite — logo is a Sun
- The stack solves one core problem: time reclamation
- Five tools with clear metaphors: Stasher (brain), Staffer (hands), Stacker (nerve center), Static (face), AutoProxy (shield)
- Stasher is the foundation — every other tool reads from it and gets smarter because it exists
- The thesis is data sovereignty: your data, your software, your agents, your rules
- Anti-SaaS positioning: other platforms trap your data in their software, SolOS flips that
